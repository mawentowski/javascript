How to Brew Pour-Over Coffee

A quick guide to consistent, tasty pour-over coffee at home.

Supplies

  • Coffee beans — 20 g
  • Water — 300 ml

Tools

  • Gooseneck kettle
  • Dripper + filter

Steps

  1. Heat water
    Bring water to ~96°C (205°F).
  2. Rinse filter
    Rinse paper filter and pre-heat the dripper.
  3. Bloom
    Pour ~40 ml to wet grounds, wait 30–45 s.
